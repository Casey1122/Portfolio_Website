About me

I graduated from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University with a major in knitwear design. Throughout my career, I have been given chances to try out different positions in the creative field, from amenity kit designer, graphic designer, visual merchandiser and even digital marketer. My enthusiasm for fascinating design, product, technology, music and movies has always provided me with food for thought. Months ago, I found myself interested in interactive design. During freelancing, I signed up with online courses and webinars to gain certain knowledge, like coding (html, css and javascript), prototyping (Figma, Adobe Xd) and UX design. I hope I can land a job in this exciting field. Oh, I also build this website from scratch!
